Hello! Welcome to my blog- MY FAVOURITES. In this blog i compile a list of my favourite people under various categories in an attempt to discover new talent as well as to meet people and form bonds with them over the similarities we share. The people that I recommend on this platform are mostly small scale and i wish that they get more recognition. So if you are interested in any of the following please try to support them through details mentioned in their social media handles and/or website. If you want to talk to new people who share the same interests as yours join the discord through this page !

Happy discovering :)
